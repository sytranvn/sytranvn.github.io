This quote is so good I have to keep it here.

September 28, 2025 One-minute read
This is a quote by Richard Sutton, the father of RL during an interview about AI.

A big issue will become corruption. If you really could just get information from anywhere and bring it into your central mind, you could become more and more powerful. And it’s all digital and they all speak some internal digital language. Maybe it’ll be easy and possible. But it might not be as easy as you imagine because you can lose your mind this way. If you pull in something from the outside and build it into your internal thinking, it could take over you, it could change you, it could be your destruction rather than your increment in knowledge.

Richard Sutton
